Peanut Butter & Jelly

Description

Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwiches are a basic option for those trying to spend more time learning to program than make food

Ingredients

  • Sliced Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Grape Jelly

Steps

  1. Take out 2 slices of bread per sandwiche desired
  2. Lay each slice out on a plate
  3. Using separate knives for Peanut Butter and Jelly, apply peanut butter to one side of half of the total bread slices.
  4. Take the slices with peanut butter and put them together with the slices with jelly. Ensure the filling is on the inside
  5. Enjoy!
